Minimum Requirements

1. A web browser. Perferably Internet Explorer 7 or better, but it depends on the websites.

2. Free Space on your hard disk.

3. An ocasional internet connection just to update the packages.

4. 800x600px Display


Questions & Answers

Supported OS's: Windows Xp onwards. But you can use Windows 9x if you'd like.

Other langauges: Not supported now in the client, but will be in the future. Also depends on the websites as well.
